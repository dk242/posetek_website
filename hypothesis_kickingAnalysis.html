What We Analyze

Every Phase of Your Kick, Quantified

We don't just watch your kick — we measure it. Every joint angle, every timing window, every force vector through the entire kinetic chain.

Approach & Plant

The Foundation of Power

Plant-foot distance, angle of approach, and hip positioning at plant determine 40% of your kick power. We measure exactly where yours stands vs. the pros.

Backswing & Hip Drive

Loading the Kinetic Chain

Hip-to-shoulder separation, knee flexion angle at peak backswing, and rotational velocity through the hips — the engine behind every powerful strike.

Contact & Follow-Through

Transferring Maximum Force

Foot speed at contact, ankle lock angle, and follow-through trajectory determine whether all that stored energy actually makes it into the ball — or leaks away.
