Quick questions

The essentials.

Where is the clinic?
Location details are sent in your confirmation email after checkout. The clinic runs from 1pm to 3pm on Tuesday, May 12, 2026.
What happens during my 5 minutes?
You'll perform a kick, a sprint, and a jump. Our team captures each one and runs the same biomechanical analysis used in the PoseTek app, then walks you through the highlights on site. Detailed quantification and downloadable-style data are not handed out at the clinic — you'll get an email within about 48 hours with a link to view everything in your PoseTek profile.
What do I need to bring?
Soccer cleats or trainers, athletic clothing, and water. We provide the cone, balls, and capture setup.
What ages is this for?
Open to youth and high school athletes. Parents are welcome on site.
Refund policy?
Full refund up to 48 hours before the clinic. After that, your slot can be transferred to another player.
Do I leave with my quantified results or data?
No — there's no report or data packet to take home from the clinic. Within about 48 hours we'll email a secure link to your PoseTek profile, where you can explore your full quantified results, analytics, and training guidance.
